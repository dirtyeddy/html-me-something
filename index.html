Snakes around St. Louis

There are several snake species around the St. Louis area, most of which are harmless, except for the timber rattlesnake and the copperhead. Most of these species are quite secretive and are seldom seen. They tend to spend most of their time hidden under rocks and cover and usually come out only to bask or to hunt at night. Over the past years I have been able to see several examples of the species listed bellow.
